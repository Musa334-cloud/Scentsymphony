Our Story

ScentSymphony was founded in 2025 with the vision of creating organic, eco-conscious perfumes that blend Africa’s natural richness with modern luxury.

Mission

To craft fragrances that connect people to nature, while upholding sustainability and ethical sourcing.

Vision

To become Africa’s leading luxury fragrance house, recognised internationally for originality and eco-luxury.
